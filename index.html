At Little Stars Bilingual Childcare Daycare, we believe that every child is a shining star with unlimited potential. Our warm, family-oriented center provides a safe, loving, and stimulating environment for children to learn, grow, and explore.

We specialize in early childhood education, helping children develop strong foundational skills while fostering creativity and curiosity. Our daily activities include age-appropriate academics, creative arts, music, outdoor play, and hands-on learning experiences.

Our Programs

Tailored learning experiences for every stage of early childhood development

Infant Care

(6 weeks - 18 months)

A warm, nurturing environment for our youngest stars with individualized attention and care.

  • Personalized feeding & sleeping schedules
  • Early language exposure through songs and stories
  • Sensory play and motor skill development

Toddler Program

(18 months - 3 years)

Encouraging exploration and independence through structured play and learning.

  • Vocabulary building
  • Potty training support
  • Social-emotional development

Preschool Program

(3 - 5 years)

Preparing children for kindergarten with a bilingual curriculum and hands-on learning.

  • Literacy and math foundations
  • Science and creative arts exploration
  • Field trips and cultural celebrations
